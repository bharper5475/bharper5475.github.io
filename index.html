Arctic Ferrets

Inheritors of the arctic continent of Keeblarium, these cuirous and nimble creatures have quite a few mysteries bundled into thier sordid histories. From the legend of Grey-Skin, the hairless arctic ferret that skinned his own kin to survive the harsh ever-winter of Nibblehiem, to the war-heros of king Ballroll's Army, these creatures have truly cemented themselves in the annals of history as a species not the be trifled with, despite thier less than threatening appearance.

STATS SKILLS DEBUFFS RULES
20 HP AGILE FIRE WEAKNESS Drinking is absolutley forbidden the entire month, and every day that the ferret does not drink they gain +1 DEVOTION. This counter is reset if the ferret drinks
40 FP COLD RESISTANCE SLOW SWIMMER Artic ferrets are able to spend accumulated DEVOTION in the ferret shop to purchase up to 3 TALISMANS
WHITE TRUEKIN OF THE FURFATHER FRAIL If an Artic ferret drinks more than 4 times, they become a "FALLEN". Fallen Ferrets Accumulate devotion for every day that they drink, and may spend thier devotion on unique TALISMANS. If the Fallen ferret does not drink, they lose thier devotion. If they do not drink 4 times in a row they turn back into Arctic ferrets.
Swamp Ferrets

These greasy, hulking masses slither hither and thither, thier nature as mysterious as the murkey water from which they hail. Not much is known of the swamp king Crimbo and his subjects, and what little is is fraught with contradictions and merrymaking. Seldom do Travelers make it back alive from trips through the trecherous gnarles of the furmire, but when they do thier stories all have one commenality; These ferrets STINK!!!

STATS SKILLS DEBUFFS RULES
30 HP STRONG COLD WEAKNESS Swamp ferrets are allowed to drink on the weekends. If a Swamp ferret decides to abstain on a weekend, they gain 1 IMMUNITY which may be used in battle to negate 1 TALISMAN
10 FP HEAT RESISTANCE FAST SWIMMER Swamp Ferrets are greasy with the slime of the swamp, and as such may be allowed to grease up a tile once per week, known only to them. If an Arctic ferret drinks on this day, there is a special random consequence.
WHITE KEEPERS OF THE LURKING FLAME HEARTY Swamp Ferrets may team up with Fallen ferrets to place traps for Arctic ferrets. If the trap is triggered the Swamp ferret may choose a free Arctic ferret TALISMAN from the shop, and the Fallen ferret gets a snack IRL
